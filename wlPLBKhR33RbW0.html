The mantra "Live like a rich man" has brought many people to a standstill. In a country where the phrase "I have a lot of money" has become a synonym of "great success" and "a happy life", it is good to know that money "works" for everyone and everyone. In this article, we will talk about how to get rid of the ever-empty wallet, improve your financial situation and find prosperity.The miracle of money — everyone is able to become rich and happy The fact is that money "loves" those who handle it correctly. A rich person is not someone who is "lucky" to be born into a rich family (having a good education and a well-developed career). The richest person in the world today is none other than Apple's Tim Cook.He has an impressive collection of knick-knacks and a slew of publications, a large personal financial capital and very decent (not to mention a winner) earnings. What you need to do for this:1) be Rich!Do you also dream of a big apartment, do you dream of traveling around the world one day and catching the "love of your life", do you dream of making a lot of money and becoming a famous person? Of course, you are dreaming! Don't sit exactly in one place!Don't have any of those who are chronically short of money.If you do not have any of the "items" listed above, then you should not even dream of an increase in your income. 2) Wealth is in the head, not in the wallet.A rich person is someone who has the means to live comfortably on a small salary. A poor person is someone who has lost all their savings in an instant, who is locked up in a Tower with an unknown number of dollars. A "victim" of circumstances does not think at all about the past and future.
